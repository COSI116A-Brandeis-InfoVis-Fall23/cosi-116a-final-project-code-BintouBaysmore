Motivation

Our research explores the different transit systems and make comparisons on cleanliness, speed, and crime rates to ensure that riders have knowledge on how their public transportation experience in different areas will be.

Data Analysis

We are focusing more on the goal of appealing to the users and allowing them to select a specific area and get information about the transits which will decide whether they will use it. Having this information provided will be greatly appreciated and have more people to use public transportation. Data provided will have the amount of people of color in the area throughout the years, the cleanliness of the station, and the crime rate in the area. 3 things most important that'll impact the usage of public transportation.
